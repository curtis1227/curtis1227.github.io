• Find a comfortable position
  • Pray for a distraction-less mind
  • Confess if needed
  • Lift up my burdens if needed
  • Breathe in the Holy Spirit and breath out all the anguish in my soul
  • Spend time in silence before the Lord. No agenda. Just be still. Waiting on him.
  • Evil will come against you. To tempt you. To break you. To lie and discourage you.
  • Cast it away. You have all authority to cast it away in Jesus Christ's name.
  • The closer you are to the truth, the more the enemy will try to drag you away.
  • The enemy has no real power, only influence.
  • Nothing can keep you from God. Jesus made sure of that. We can always reach him.
  • Repent if temptations seep into your mind. Repent. Receive. Rebuke. Replace.
